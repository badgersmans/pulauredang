Frequently Asked Questions

How would I get to the jetty?
If you are in the car, head straight to the Municipal / Merang jetty. Car parking is available at RM10 / day. If you board a flight, you can fly to Sultan Mahmud airport. Then take the airport transfer to the jetty for different charges depending on the type of transport. To catch a bus, you can stop at the Kuala Terengganu bus stop. Then you can walk to the pier for about 5 minutes.

What if I wanted to have a honeymoon?
Sorry there are no special packages for honeymoon.
But you could still take our package as usual. The only difference is no bed decoration & candle light dinner.
Is it safe for pregnant mothers to join the snorkeling trip?
Depending on the level of maternal health and maturity of the womb. Mothers who are 6 months or older are advised not to join.
For those who are less than 6 months old, you could join the snorkelling trip. During your trip you don't have to swim, you can follow and enjoy the beauty of the island and the sea from the top of the boat.
Do I get refunded if there is bad weather?
No, because all service and tour fees were payed before the trip began.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes, pay the deposit of RM 100 first then subsequent payments will be in installments.Full payment must be made 30 days before your trip date.
What if I have medical complications?
You are advised to seek medical approval from your doctor before the trip, should your doctor deem you unfit, we will issue you a full refund under this circustance.
About

Pulau redang is one of the largest islands off the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia. It is famous for its crystal clear waters and white sandy beaches. It is one of nine islands, which form a marine sanctuary park offering snorkeling and diving opportunities for tourists.
